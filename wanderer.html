Wanderer

Wandering her ways through an unfamiliar memory
A memory too vivid yet blur at times
An echo from miles and miles away
Calling her name for something blue
How does she run with a body so numb and a mind so lost?
It was you
a familiar face,
Do I know you? she asked
She did
She couldn’t run
She didn’t know to
You wonder what she saw
But it was one of you she saw
