Death

When one experiences the most unbearable pain in their life, they say I felt like dying
well, of course, it is bearable, but one cannot help but feel that it's unendurable
But that is not what we want to argue
the metaphor between death and pain is unjust
Death is something one has never experienced until one is gone
So how could one know “it felt like dying”?
And death is better than those momentary sufferings
Because once you are gone, then there is no pain, no anguish, no disappointments
Because one doesn’t feel anymore
One is not clung to the idea to be loved, to be seen, to feel content
But dear, what would I know? I haven’t experienced death as well
But only wished to at times
