Be anything but...

Has life given you anything more than pain?
maybe it has only tied you to several chains
ones you have not chosen
all your wishes have gone to vain
but dear don’t you complain
because you are still on the train
live in the illusion of happiness now and again
the world’s inhumane but so are you
one lesson friend, be anything but mundane
