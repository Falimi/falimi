Home

A question I asked myself,
Where is home?
A place where I was born or a place where I lived?
Is it a place I long for?
Or is it someone I love?
Home, where are you?
Do you exist or are you just a metaphor, a word?
I miss home,
But I do not know where home is
I miss the smell of home,
But I do not remember what home smells like,
Are you my mother or my father?
Are you my sense of comfort?
Today I’ve become homeless,
Thinking whether I’ll ever find you,
I know what it feels like to be home,
But I do not know what or where home is,
A question I asked myself again,
Will it pass? Will I find my home?
I’ve wandered too long now,
Too long to remember home,
Where is home after all?
Home is nowhere but everywhere,
If you’re lucky you’ll have one
But if not, you’ll wander the rest of your days looking for one.
